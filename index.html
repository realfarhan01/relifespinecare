    Understanding Your Body is a guide to back pain, sciatica, slip discs, neck pain, nerve tingling, and knee swelling.

    Back Pain

    Back pain can range from dull aches to sharp, stabbing sensations. Causes include muscle strain, herniated discs, and spinal abnormalities.

    Sciatica

    Sciatica symptoms can affect your lower back, buttocks, and legs. Pressure on the spinal cord or nerves is often the cause.

    Slip Disc

    A herniated or slipped disk in the spine can cause neck, back, or leg pain. You can often treat it with self-care at home.

    Cervical Pain

    Cervical spondylosis affects the joints and discs in your neck, causing pain, stiffness, and other uncomfortable symptoms.

    The Tingling Sensation in the Nerves

    Tingling Nerve Sensations: Exploring Causes and Remedies. In general, tingling can happen when there's pressure, trauma, or damage to nerves.

    Swelling and pain in the knees

    Knee swelling occurs when fluid builds up in your knee joint. It can happen because of traumas, like sports injuries. Or health conditions, like arthritis.

    Treatment Protocol

    Spinal Adjustment Therapy

    Spinal adjustment therapy, also known as chiropractic adjustment, is a hands-on, non-invasive form of therapy that aims to improve spinal alignment and function.

    PNF / Met Therapy

    PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation) and MET (Muscle Energy Technique) Therapy are two popular forms of physical therapy that are often used in Jaipur to treat a variety of musculoskeletal conditions.

    Kochi Therapy

    Kochi therapy is a popular form of massage that originated in the city of Kochi in Kerala, India.

    Dry Needling Therapy

    Dry needling therapy is a technique used by healthcare practitioners to treat musculoskeletal pain and dysfunction.

    Cupping Therapy

    Cupping therapy is a traditional healing practice that involves placing cups on the skin to create a suction effect.

    Exercise Therapy

    Therapeutic exercises refer to a range of physical activities that are designed to help individuals recover from injury, improve their mobility, or manage chronic health conditions.

    Chiropractic Therapy

    If you’re looking for chiropractic treatment in Jaipur, it’s important to find the best chiropractor in Jaipur.

    Electro Therapy

    Doctors in Jaipur are now utilizing electrical stimulation as a promising therapy for patients with a range of medical conditions.

    Osteopathy Therapy

    If you’re looking for osteopathy therapy treatment in Jaipur, there are several options available.

    Magnetic Therapy

    Magnetic therapy, also known as magneto therapy, is a complementary medicine practice that uses magnetic fields to help improve health and treat certain conditions.

    Myofascial release Therapy

    Myofascial release therapy is a popular treatment option for those suffering from muscle pain and tension.

    Decompression Therapy

    Spinal Decompression therapy in Jaipur is a non-invasive treatment approach that aims to alleviate back pain and other symptoms related to spinal disc compression.
